\caption{\textbf{Where biology should enter the router.} We ablate the depth router under
the same 5-fold CV on Baron (single-cell) and Prostate (multi-omics), comparing a fixed
\emph{biology-only} graph, a \emph{data-only} learned graph, and their combination
(\emph{biology $+$ data}, bioMoR). The data-only learned graph already matches or beats the
fixed biology-only graph, and combining the two is best---biological structure helps only
when it is learned jointly with the data, not imposed.}
\label{fig:biorouter}
\end{figure}
